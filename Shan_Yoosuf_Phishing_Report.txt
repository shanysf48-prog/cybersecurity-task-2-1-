Phishing Email Analysis Report Submitted By: Shan Yoosuf Internship:
Cyber Security Internship - Task 2

Objective: To analyze a suspicious email sample and identify phishing
characteristics.

Sample Email Summary: The email claims to be from a bank requesting
urgent verification of account details due to suspicious activity.

Phishing Indicators Identified: 1. Spoofed sender email address. 2.
Urgent and threatening language. 3. Suspicious hyperlink redirecting to
fake login page. 4. Mismatched URL. 5. Spelling and grammar errors. 6.
Request for sensitive information. 7. Header discrepancies detected.

Header Analysis Findings: SPF and DKIM checks failed. Return-path does
not match sender domain.

Risks: - Credential theft - Financial loss - Identity theft - Malware
infection

Conclusion: The email contains clear phishing traits and should be
reported and deleted immediately.
